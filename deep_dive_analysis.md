# Deep Dive Analysis – Bank Loan Report

## 📊 Objective
To analyze loan performance and identify patterns in loan status, state distribution, and loan purpose.

---

## 🔍 1. Loan Status Analysis (Good vs Bad)

- Good Loans: Fully Paid + Current
- Bad Loans: Charged Off

### Insights:
- Majority loans are good loans
- Bad loans represent a smaller percentage but indicate risk areas
